FAQ

Questions, answered

How long does it take to get set up?
Most teams are live in under five minutes. Connect a source, and Lumen auto-detects your schema and generates starter dashboards instantly — no data engineer required.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. Lumen is SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR-compliant, with encryption in transit and at rest, SSO/SAML, granular role-based access, and optional custom data residency on Enterprise.
Can I try it before paying?
Yes — there's a free-forever Starter plan, plus a 14-day Pro trial with no credit card required. Cancel anytime, no questions asked.
Which tools does Lumen integrate with?
Over 200 integrations including Snowflake, BigQuery, Postgres, Segment, Stripe, Salesforce, HubSpot, and more. Plus a clean REST API and SDKs for custom events.
Do you offer discounts for startups or nonprofits?
We do. Eligible early-stage startups and registered nonprofits get up to 50% off Pro. Reach out to our team and we'll get you set up.
